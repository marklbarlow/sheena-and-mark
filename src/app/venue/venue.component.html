Directions

By car: From the M11, take the A120 westbound and exit at the roundabout signposted B1383 Newport. Then take directions via Stansted Mountfitchet. After driving through the village of Quendon the entrance to Parklands is immediately on your left as you exit the village. There is plenty of parking on the grounds.

By rail: Newport is the nearest station to Parklands, Quendon Hall, about 2 miles away. A taxi will take about 5 minutes to reach the venue. The station is on the London (Liverpool Street) to Cambridge line.
